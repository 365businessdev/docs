Welcome to the documentation for 365 business development Extension License! This guide provides you with all the information you need to understand and independently carry out the licensing of 365 business development apps and extensions.

## About 365 business development Extension License

The licensing of 365 business development GmbH extensions and apps is usually done via the dependent extension "365 business development Extension License" (formerly "Extension License Manager"). Licensing actions take place directly in Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central.

This documentation describes how to license an extension of 365 business development GmbH.

<div class="alert alert-notice">
    <i class="fa-solid fa-notes"></i> <strong>Note:</strong> With the release of the extension "365 business development Extension License" (from version 2.0.0.0), additional licensing methods, as well as the inclusion of 365 business API calls, are supported.</a>.
</div>

## Overview

365 business development Extension License is the extension of 365 business development for managing licenses for extensions and apps. The extension offers the following functions:

- **Trial period**: Automatic activation of extensions and apps after installation and management of the trial period.

- **Extension of the trial period**: One-time option to extend the 30-day trial period of extensions and apps by another **10 days**.

- **License activation**: Activation of the license for the extensions and apps of 365 business development GmbH.

- **License termination**: Termination or ending of the license for the extensions and apps of 365 business development GmbH.

- **Invoices**: View the invoices for your end customer subscription and retrieve the invoices as a PDF file.

- **Expenditure limit for API transactions**: Setting expenditure limits for additional API transactions that go beyond your included API transactions.
