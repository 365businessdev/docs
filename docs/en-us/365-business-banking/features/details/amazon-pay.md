# Amazon Pay Integration

## Business Value

Amazon Pay is a service that allows you to use the payment methods already stored in your Amazon account to pay for goods or services on third-party websites. The integration of Amazon Pay into 365 business Banking enables companies to import and manage payment transactions processed through Amazon Pay directly in Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central.

## Feature Description

With the Amazon Pay Integration in 365 business Banking, you can import and reconcile payment transactions processed through Amazon Pay directly in Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central in the Payment Reconciliation Journal. The Amazon Pay account is set up as a **Bank Account** in Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central and connected to the **[Payment Service](../../payment-services.md)** via the account connection of 365 business Banking.
