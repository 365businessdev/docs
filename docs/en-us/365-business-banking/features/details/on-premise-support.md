# Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central On-Premise Deployment

## Business Value

Some companies prefer to host their ERP systems locally (On-Premise) rather than using cloud-based solutions. The support for Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central On-Premise deployment by 365 business Banking enables these companies to leverage the benefits of bank account integration and payment services while managing their data and systems internally.

## Feature Description

365 business Banking supports all versions of Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central, starting from version 2021 Release Wave 1 (BC18). Companies using Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central On-Premise can seamlessly integrate 365 business Banking to manage bank accounts, import payment transactions, and utilize payment services. The integration includes setting up bank accounts, synchronizing transactions, and using payment services to efficiently manage payment processes.

All features and benefits of 365 business Banking are also available to On-Premise users, allowing them to benefit from a comprehensive solution for banking and payment management without relying on the cloud.
