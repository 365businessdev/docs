# Shopify Payments Integration

## Business Value

Through Microsoft's Shopify integration, Shopify is a popular webshop provider when Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central is used as an ERP system. Shopify Payments is a popular payment service provider (PSP) that enables businesses to process payments seamlessly through their Shopify webshops. The integration of Shopify Payments into 365 business Banking provides businesses with the ability to import and manage payment transactions directly in Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central.

## Feature Description

With the Shopify Payments integration in 365 business Banking, you can import and reconcile payment transactions processed through Shopify Payments directly in Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central, in the payment reconciliation journal. The Shopify Payments account is set up as a **bank account** in Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central and connected to the **[Payment Service](../../payment-services.md)** via the account connection of 365 business Banking.
