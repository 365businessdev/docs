# Mollie

Mollie is a payment service provider that enables businesses to accept payments online. With Mollie you can integrate various payment methods such as credit cards, PayPal, SEPA Direct Debit, and many others. With **365 business Banking** you can connect your Mollie account directly to **Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central** to automatically fetch transactions and include them in payment reconciliation.

## Prerequisites

- An active Mollie account.

## Step-by-step instructions

1. Select **Bank Accounts** from the Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central search.
2. Create a new bank account or select an existing one.
3. On the **Bank Account Card**, under **Actions**, choose the **Account Connection** action group and select **Connectâ€¦**.
   ![Start connection](/assets/images/365-business-banking/connect-banking-provider.de-DE.png)
4. Select **Mollie** from the list.
5. Confirm the prompt by selecting **Yes**.
6. Click **Authenticate** to establish a connection between Mollie Connect and 365 business Banking.
7. Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the authentication.
   ![Authenticate Mollie](/assets/images/365-business-banking/mollie-connect.de-DE.png)

## Disconnect account connection

1. Select **Bank Accounts** from the Microsoft Dynamics 365 Business Central search.
2. Select the bank account whose connection you want to disconnect.
3. On the **Bank Account Card**, under **Actions**, choose the **Account Connection** action group and select **Disconnect...**.
   ![Disconnect connection](/assets/images/365-business-banking/disconnect-banking-provider.de-DE.png)
4. Confirm the prompt by selecting **Yes**.
